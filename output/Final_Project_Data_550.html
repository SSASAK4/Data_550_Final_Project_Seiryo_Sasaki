Introduction

This report analyzes a global data set encompassing 204 countries, covering key trends in economics, education, public health, and the environment.

The primary objective is to evaluate regional disparities across critical development indicators, specifically GDP, life expectancy, unemployment, homicide rates, CO2 emissions, and tourism. By identifying these gaps, the report aims to highlight how different geographic areas diverge in their developmental trajectories.
